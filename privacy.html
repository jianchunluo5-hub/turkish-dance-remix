This Privacy Policy explains how Turkish Dance Remix handles information when users connect TikTok or use the workflow tool.

The tool may process account authorization information, basic profile information, video workflow metadata, and files that the user chooses to review or post.

Information is used to operate authorized account login, content review, music matching, and posting workflows. We do not sell personal data.

We may share information with platform APIs only as needed to provide the requested integration. We do not share user data with unrelated third parties for advertising.

Users may revoke TikTok access through TikTok settings or request deletion of stored workflow information by contacting us.
